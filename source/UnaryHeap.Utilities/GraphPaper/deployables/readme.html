GraphPaper

GraphPaper is a what-you-see-is-what-you-get (WYSIWYG) editing program that can create and edit UnaryHeap.Utilities.Graph2D objects.

The editor is used by clicking and dragging on the graph paper displayed, with various modifier keys pressed. The following click and drag operations are supported:

Left-clickRight-click
No modifier keysSelect a vertex or edge
ShiftAdd a vertex or edge to current selection
ControlToggle selection of a vertex or edgeAdd vertex to graph
Control+ShiftSplit an edge in half with a new vertex
