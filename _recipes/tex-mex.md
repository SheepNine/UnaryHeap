## Ingredients

(ingredients listed as ratios, to simplify bulk preparation)

* chili powder (6)
* paprika (4)
* cumin (4)
* garlic powder (2)
* onion powder (2)
* chili flakes (1)
* oregano (1)
* black pepper (1)

There are 21 units in total. If you use tbsp, you will make a bit less than 4/3 cups.

## Instructions

Mix all the ingredients. Store in a jar.
